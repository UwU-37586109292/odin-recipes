My nana's lasagna recipe

Description

I remember when in 1912 my Nana decided to make lasanga... This has been the best day of my life.

Since I'm such a great friend I would like to share this recipe with you!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Lasagne pasta
  • Minced meat
  • Fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Fry minced meat
  2. Preheat oven to 180*C
  3. Add tomatoes to minced meat
  4. Put pasta into a dish and add tomato sauce
  5. Put in oven for 25 minutes
