My Spaghetti Carbonara recipe

Description

I remember when just moved out from my parents... This has been the best day of my life.

Since I'm such a great friend I would like to share this recipe with you!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • 30% fat cream
  • Parmegianno Regano
  • Fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Cook spaghetti (al dente)
  3. Add Parmegianno Regano to pasta
  4. Serve with fresh herbs
