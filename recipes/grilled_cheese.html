Grilled cheese recipe

Description

Best dish when you're in a pickle! Simple ingredients, cheap, tasty!

Eat freshly done, otherwise what's the point?

Ingredients

  • Bread (2pcs)
  • Cheese (At least 2 types)

Steps

  1. Put bread on high heat frying pan
  2. Put cheese on the bread piece
  3. When cheese is melted add second piece of bread and flip
  4. When both sides are nice and crispy it's ready to eat!
